A team of Royan herders has run into trouble while guiding their Exoswarm across the frostlands. Now, they need your help to track down the strays.

Duration

2026-02-19 04:00 - 2026-03-09 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x400, Premium Resonance Potion x9, Premium Energy Core x6, Premium Sealed Tube x6, Forgery Advanced Supply - Lahai-Roi x18, Advanced Enclosure Tank Lahai-Roi x36, Shell Credit x240000

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14 and unlock the Resonance Nexus of the Startorch Academy during Main Quest Chapter III Act I ""What Burns Beneath Frostlands"" to unlock.

Event Rules

  • During the event, ""Migration Mayhem,"" stages unlock daily, featuring both Easy and Hard difficulty modes. The Easy and Hard difficulties each include 1 and 2 lost Exoswarm respectively.

  • During the challenge, players and Exoswarm take turns to move: Place the Royan herder's herding staff on the frostlands as obstacles, use various terrain conditions such as sinkholes and ice walls, etc., to restrict the Exoswarm's movement and surround them, and ultimately guide the lost Exoswarm back to the migration pack.

  • Completely surround the Exoswarm to complete the stage.
