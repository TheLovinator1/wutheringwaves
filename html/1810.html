Jinhsi's Premium Outfit "Peach Blossom" will become available when Version 2.0 goes live. Let's take a look together!

How to Obtain

Jinhsi's Premium Outfit "Peach Blossom" will be made permanently available at the Outfits Store after Version 2.0 goes live.

Purchase the outfit before the Version 2.1 update at a discount, for 2680 Lunites only. After Version 2.1 goes live, the outfit will cost 3280 Lunites.

From the time Version 2.0 goes live until 2025-02-12 11:59 (server time), there will be a trial event where you can try out Jinhsi's new Peach Blossom outfit. Complete the trial event to claim rewards.
