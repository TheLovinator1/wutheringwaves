Arno, the special agent of the Black Shores, is currently looking for assistants to collect the Surveillance Datasets on the Lament with the promise of generous rewards. If you are interested, go talk to him.

Duration

2024-10-10 10:00 - 2024-10-31 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x400, Forgery Advanced Supply x21, Advanced Sealed Tube x16, Advanced Resonance Potion x20, Shell Credit x560,000 and other development materials.

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14 and complete Main Quest Chapter I Act VII "Thaw of Eons."

Details

  • Once the Detection Point is unlocked, you can head to the designated area to start the challenge.

  • After the challenge starts, Surveillance Datasets will appear. Try to collect as many Surveillance Datasets as possible within the time limit.

  • Once all existing Datasets are collected or time runs out, rewards will be calculated based on the number of Datasets collected. The more Datasets you collect, the better the rewards.

  • If you have any unclaimed rewards when the event ends, you can log in within 30 days afterward to receive these rewards via mail.
