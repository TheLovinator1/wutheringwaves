The following base drop rates represent the mathematical chance of obtaining related items with a single convene. The average drop rates (including guaranteed drops) are derived from base drop rates and the guarantee mechanism, based on extensive sample/user testing. These rates may differ from the convene records of an individual player. Please spend responsibly.

Utterance of Marvels Convene Rules

  • [Utterance of Marvels] is an event banner available for new players.

  • Use [Lustrous Tide] to Convene. You are guaranteed to obtain at least one Resonator or Weapon of 4-star or higher rarity every 10 attempts.

  • You can Convene up to 50 times in the [Utterance of Marvels] event banner. The event banner is available until you have obtained a 5-star Resonator, after which it will be closed.

  • After the [Utterance of Marvels] event banner has closed, the [Beginner's Choice Convene] event banner will become available.

  • [Utterance of Marvels] has separate Guarantee counts from other Convene event banners.

Rates

  • The base 5-star Resonator drop rate is 0.8%. The average drop rate (including the Guarantee) is 2.4%. You are guaranteed to obtain at least one 5-star Resonator every 50 Convenes.

  • The base 4-star Resonator or Weapon drop rate is 6.0%. The average drop rate (including the Guarantee) is 11.8%. You are guaranteed to obtain at least one Resonator or Weapon of 4-star or higher rarity every 10 Convenes.

  • The base 3-star Weapon drop rate is 93.2%.

Extra Rewards

  • On obtaining a 5-star Resonator for the first time, you will get 15 Afterglow Corals. On obtaining a 5-star Resonator that you already own for the 2nd – 7th time, you will get 1 Waveband of that Resonator and 15 Afterglow Corals. From the 8th time onward, you will get 40 Afterglow Corals.

  • On obtaining a 4-star Resonator for the first time, you will get 3 Afterglow Corals. On obtaining a 4-star Resonator that you already own for the 2nd – 7th time, you will get 1 Waveband of that Resonator and 3 Afterglow Corals. From the 8th time onward, you will get 8 Afterglow Corals.

  • Whenever you obtain a 4-star Weapon, you will get 3 Afterglow Corals.

  • Whenever you obtain a 3-star Weapon, you will get 15 Oscillated Corals.

Notes

  • You may redeem up to 2 limited stock Wavebands for each 5-star Resonator with Afterglow Corals.

  • You may redeem various items with Oscillate Corals. Please visit the in-game Store page for current options and redemption limits, reset with each version update.
