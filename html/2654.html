During the event, 5-Star Weapons: Ages of Harvest, Blazing Brilliance, The Last Dance, Tragicomedy, Unflickering Valor, and 4-Star Weapons: Undying Flame, Variation, Relativistic Jet receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

2025-05-22 10:00 - 2025-06-11 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the combat trial in ""First Resonance"" and activate the Convene function.

Convene Rules

  • This is an Anniversary Weapon Convene event banner. Use Forging Tide to convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or Weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The Guarantee count is shared among all [Anniversary Weapon Convene] and [Featured Weapon Convene] event banners. If a 5-Star Weapon is not obtained in this event banner, the Guarantee count is carried over across other [Anniversary Weapon Convene] and [Featured Weapon Convene] event banners and resets once a 5-Star Weapon is obtained.

  • You can select one 5-Star Weapon within a designated range as the Featured Weapon of the event banner (you can re-select at any time). The 5-Star Weapon you Convene is guaranteed to the selected 5-Star Weapon.

  • Selectable 5-Star Weapons: Ages of Harvest, Blazing Brilliance, The Last Dance, Tragicomedy, Unflickering Valor

For more details, please refer to the in-game Convene page.
