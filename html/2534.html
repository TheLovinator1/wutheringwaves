During the event, 5-Star Weapon: Blazing Justice, 4-Star Weapons: Dauntless Evernight, Overture, and Hollow Mirage receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

Version 2.3 update - 2025-05-22 09:59 (server time)

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the combat trial in ""First Resonance"" and activate the Convene function.

Convene Rules

  • [Absolute Pulsation] is a Featured Weapon Convene event. Use Forging Tide to convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or Weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The Guarantee count is shared among all [Anniversary Weapon Convene] and [Featured Weapon Convene] event banners. If a 5-Star Weapon is not obtained in this event banner, the Guarantee count is carried over across other [Anniversary Weapon Convene] and [Featured Weapon Convene] event banners and resets once a 5-Star Weapon is obtained.

  • The 5-Star Weapon you Convene is guaranteed to be Blazing Justice.

  • Featured Weapon Blazing Justice is exclusive to the Featured Weapon Convene and will not be available by other means once the event ends.

For more details, please refer to the in-game Convene page.
