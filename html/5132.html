The Land of Xuanfang lies wedged between Jinzhou and Mengzhou, its formidable Xuanfang Hold containing much of the chaos unleashed by the Norfall Barrens' TD Outbreak. Xuanfang Hold stands as the ultimate destination for Automata Mechanics, pulsing under the watchful control of the colossal Celestial Ring Formation, a belt encircling the entire fortress. When threats arise, the city responds with ingenious creations known as Autopuppets. Bricks and tiles twist and rearrange themselves in an instant, while beams and pillars assemble into cages that trap monsters or transform into Autopuppets that eliminate them.
