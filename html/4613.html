Features

**

Expedition Motorbike:**

Added Aerial mode

  • As you progress through the Main Quest, the motorbike's aerial capability will unlock. At a certain height, expend Aerial Energy to enter the gliding state.

  • While gliding, you can consume Boost Energy to dash. The motorbike will not descend during the dash.

Added Expansion Module "Aerial Tactics"

  • As you progress through the Main Quests, new Expansion Modules, which provide various buffs, will be unlocked for the expedition motorbike. The "Aerial Tactics" Expansion Module will be unlocked during Version 3.3's Main Quest.

  • To switch between Expansion Modules, go to the Motorbike Expansion Module page or the Expansion Module Switch of the Vehicle section of the Utility wheel. Only one Expansion Module can be active at a time. Core Module remains effective when switching between different tactic modules, while Combat Modules change with the selected Expansion Module.

  • Unlock Charged Leap - Core Module on the Expansion Module screen. After unlocking it, hold the Bounce Jump button to use the motorbike's Charged Leap to gain height. Charged Leap has a maximum of 3 charges, which replenish over time while the motorbike is on the ground.

New customizable vehicle looks

  • Added rear seat Accessories.

  • View and change Motorbike Accessories via Expedition Motorbike > Motorbike Appearance.

Motorbike Accessories placed on the rear seat will be temporarily hidden in situations such as Ride Sharing or when the rear seat is occupied.

Adjusted handling of Bounce Jump

  • With this update, riders can now perform a second Bounce Jump before both wheels touch down.

Enhanced expedition motorbike lighting effects

  • The shimmer around the bike will now shift with different chassis materials, resulting in a more three-dimensional visual presentation.

Refined Cinematic Mode

  • Refined Cinematic Mode with improved, more dynamic camera moves.

Graphics & Performance

**

Graphics Settings**

Added Anisotropic Filtering (PC, Mac, Mobile)

  • Access "Anisotropic Filtering" via Settings > Graphics > Anisotropic Filtering. You can select one of the following 5 settings: 1x, 2x, 4x, 8x, 16x; restart the game for the change to take effect.

  • Enable "Anisotropic Filtering" to improve texture quality, making surfaces, such as grounds and walls, appear sharper and enhancing the overall visual clarity of distant objects. The higher the setting, the more evident the effect.

**

PC Graphics**

Added DLAA on some PCs

  • With NVDIA DLSS enabled, select the "DLAA" option via Settings > Graphics > Super Resolution.

  • Enabling DLAA allows the game to deliver finer image quality with greater visual detail when rendered at native resolution.

  • Enabling DLAA may contribute significantly to the device load. Please choose a suitable setting based on the performance of your device.

The "Ultra Quality" Super Resolution option will be removed following the Version 3.3 update. If you select this option before the update, the "Auto" option will be selected instead following the update.

  • Added environmental interactions on PC for when Resonators' blades cut through grass in Dimmr Plains.

  • Added environmental interactions on PC to show snow being knocked off bushes in Dimmr Plains and Frostlands Surface.

  • Optimized some audio/stereo effects.

**

Beta Feature**

Introducing self-developed frame generation on some mobile devices

  • To ensure the stable running of the game, during Version 3.3, the self-developed frame generation will be made available only to mobile devices equipped with the following processors: Snapdragon 8s Gen 4, Snapdragon 8 Gen 3, Snapdragon 8 Gen 2.

  • Testing schedule

  1. The self-developed frame generation will be made available to Snapdragon 8s Gen 4 through a hotfix during Version 3.3.

  2. Based on the performance of this feature on Snapdragon 8s Gen 4, it will be decided whether or not to make it also available to devices equipped with the other processors.

A notice will be released in this regard during the testing period. Please remember to check the notice titled [Version 3.3 Known Issues & Updates].

  • If the processor of your device meets the requirement, you can enable this option via Settings > Graphics > Frame Generation.

  • Running the game with the "Frame Generation" option enabled (up to 60FPS) consumes less power than running the game on 60FPS without the option enabled.

Due to variations in the in-game settings and system environment, enabling the option may occasionally cause performance issues, in-game glitches, and hardware-related issues. If you encounter an issue, please try switching to a lower setting. If the issue persists, please contact customer support for further assistance.
