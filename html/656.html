Rules (Artwork)

♦ How To Participate

  1. Follow our official X/Twitter or Instagram account.

  2. Post your very own Wuthering Waves fan art with #WutheringWaves and #FanCreation hashtags on X/Twitter or Instagram.

  3. After posting your work to the aforementioned platforms, submit your artwork file to our official link before the end of the event period for judges' review: forms.gle/dPDNe29HppZGiSLc6

  4. Multiple entries are permitted. Only one prize can be won per participant.

  5. Entries not posted and submitted to our official link within the event period will be considered invalid entries.

  6. Winners will be decided by official event judges at Kuro Games.

♦ Participating Platforms

X/Twitter, Instagram

First Prize (1 Winner): 500 USD

Second Prize (2 Winners): 200 USD

Third Prize (5 Winners): 100 USD

Excellence Prize (10 Winners): Official Merchandise x1

Original fan art related to Wuthering Waves.

Rules (Video)

♦ How To Participate

  1. Follow our official YouTube or TikTok channel.

  2. Post your very own Wuthering Waves video(s) on YouTube/TikTok with #WutheringWaves and #FanCreation hashtags
