Graphics & Performance

**

Graphics Settings**

Added XeSS2 frame generation for Intel graphics cards

  • This option is available for all discrete Intel graphics cards.

Added FSR 3 frame generation for AMD graphics cards

To enable this option, your device must meet the two requirements listed below:

  1. AMD RX 6000 or newer.

  2. With DX12 enabled.

AMD RX 5000 Series graphics cards only support FSR 3 without frame generation.

NVIDIA DLSS 4 with Multi Frame Generation is now supported, along with the previous versions.

To enable DLSS 4, your device must meet the two requirements listed below:

  1. RTX 50 Series

  2. With DX12 enabled (first enable Hardware-Accelerated GPU Scheduling in your system's graphics settings).

  • If your device meets the requirements, you can enable the option via Settings > Graphics > Advanced > Frame Generation.

  • If your device meets the DLSS 4 requirements, you can select 2x, 3x, or 4x Frame Generation based on your needs or the performance of your device.

If the original frame rate is too low, enabling Frame Generation can increase the frame rate. Please note that enabling this option may cause input lag or display issues.
