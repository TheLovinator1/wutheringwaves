[How to Update]

Once the maintenance for the version update begins, you can follow the following instructions to update your client.

✦PC: Close the game, restart the launcher, and then click on "Update."

✦Android: Launch the game then follow the instructions in the update pop-up window.

✦iOS: Update the game in the App Store.

✦PS5™: Open the home screen > Select Wuthering Waves > Press the OPTIONS button to Check for Update

[PC Pre-download]

✦PC Pre-download Start Time: 2025-02-11 09:30 (UTC+8)

With the pre-download feature enabled, "New Version Pre-download" appears below the "Start" button on the Launcher interface. Select this option to pre-download the material for the latest version. You can continue playing the game while the pre-downloading process is ongoing. The process can be paused at any time. Whether it is complete or not, the update maintenance will speed up to a certain degree when initiated.

The pre-download package is about 45 GB in size. To ensure smoother decompressing and installing processes, it is recommended that you have at least 60 GB of spare storage space on your PC.
