Official Guide Depot**

New Feature: In-game Guide Depot

  • The official Guide Depot will become available in Version 2.4. Press the Guide Depot icon on the Terminal page to access this feature.

  • The Guide Depot offers guides on how to build Resonators efficiently. On the first page, you can get brief versions of the guides.

  • Press the avatar of a specific Resonator to access the corresponding guide, which gives recommendations on Echoes, upgrade priority, Resonance Chain nodes, weapons, teammates, and more.

  • If there are multiple guides for the same Resonator, you have the option of switching between them.

  • Moreover, you can judge how far along you have built a specific Resonator based on the stats recommended by the author of a particular guide.

Please note that data refreshes may be delayed.

Solaris New Voyage Celebration**

New Feature: Solaris New Voyage Celebration

  • Create a character profile after the Version 2.4 update and reach Union Level 5. The Solaris New Voyage Celebration unlocks once you meet the requirements. The event lasts 30 days.

  • The event has four types of tasks: Tidal Memories, Combat Scenes, Scenic Wonders, and Growth Roadmap. Complete these tasks to earn item rewards and Celebration Festivity Points.

  • Accumulate Festivity Points to unlock valuable rewards. Keep building it up to claim a standard 5-star Resonator of your choice!

  • When the Solaris New Voyage Celebration unlocks, the events "New Voyage Bundles" and "New Voyage Convene" will also unlock.

  • The events "New Voyage Bundles" and "New Voyage Convene" last as long as the Solaris New Voyage Celebration.

  • New Voyage Convene consists of New Voyage Companions: New Voyage Resonator Convene and New Voyage Arms: New Voyage Weapon Convene. During this event, you can select a Resonator/weapon from Versions 1.0 to 1.4 as your Convene target.

For more details about New Voyage Convene, please refer to the Convene page.

Graphics & Performance

**

Graphics Settings**

New Feature: Global Filter

  • Access the feature via Settings > Graphics > Global Filter on PC, PS5™, or Mac.

  • Six styles of Global Filter are available: Default, Vivid, Dystopian, Film, Noir, Wild. You may fine-tune the intensity and visual effects of the selected Global Filter.

  • Please note that cutscenes and UI elements will not be affected by the filter you select.

With a Global Filter applied, the screen may appear too bright or too dim under certain settings. Playing the game under such conditions for long periods of time may contribute to dry eye. Please keep this in mind while enjoying the game. We will continue to improve this feature in future versions.

New Graphics Quality option: Ultra Quality

To enable the Ultra Quality option, your device must meet the two requirements listed below:

  1. NVIDIA RTX 4080 or newer

  2. With a CPU capable of running 120 FPS

  • If your device meets the requirements, you can enable Ultra Quality via Settings > Graphics > Graphics Quality.

  • With this option enabled, some models will be shown in greater detail, distance ranges at which certain details are shown will be greater, the shadow quality will improve, and more lights will be displayed.

  • With this option enabled, models in some surroundings of Septimont will be shown in higher resolution, and the texturing quality will also improve.

  • Added the following effect on PC: Casting skills while in the water produces ripples on the surface.

  • Added the following effect on PC: Flying close to the water produces ripples and sprays on the surface.

  • Added the following effect on PC: Casting skills near fallen leaves in Septimont stirs the leaves.

  • Added the following effect on PC: In Septimont, the ground changes from dry to wet when it rains or wet to dry when it stops raining.

  • Extended the availability of the 120 FPS option to Mac devices equipped with the following chips: M3 Pro, M3 Max, M4, M4 Pro.

  • Extended the availability of the 90 FPS option to the following Android devices: RedMagic 10 Pro, RedMagic 10 Pro+, RedMagic 10S Pro, RedMagic 10S Pro+. Enable the 90 FPS option via Settings > Graphics > Frame Rate for a smoother gaming experience.

  • Adjusted the resolution settings on Mac (accessed via Settings > Graphics > Basic > Resolution). After Version 2.4 goes live, changes to the resolution within the game are not allowed in Full Screen mode. If you want to change the in-game resolution, please adjust the resolution of your device.

  • Adjusted the applicability of the Crowd Density option (accessed via Settings > Graphics > Crowd Density). After Version 2.4 goes live, "Low" will be selected by default for devices with less RAM and the Crowd Density option will be hidden.
