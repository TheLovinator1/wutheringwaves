New Utility: Expedition Motorbike**

The expedition motorbike is a lightweight, all-terrain vehicle co-developed by the Department of Exostrider Engineering and the Department of Voidmatters at Startorch Academy. You can summon the expedition motorbike or adjust the relevant settings via the Utility wheel. The motorbike cannot be summoned while in combat or when there is insufficient space.

[Adjustments and Optimizations]

Resonators & Weapons

**

Combat Mechanics**

New combat mechanic: Tune Break

  • Click on the Tune Break icon on a Resonator's Forte page for more details.

  • Your Resonators' attacks build up enemies' Off-Tune Level. Once the enemy's Off-Tune Level reaches the limit, they enter the Mistune state. During the enemy's Mistune state, your Resonators can unleash a powerful Tune Break, dealing additional damage. Landing a Tune Break resets the enemies' Off-Tune Level. Within a period after being struck by Tune Break, the enemy's Off-Tune level cannot be built up again.

**

Skills**

New feature: Resonance Mode Switch

  • The Forte pages of certain Resonators now feature a Resonance Mode Switch icon. Click on the icon to switch between Resonance Modes and change the combat characteristics.

  • A Resonance Mode icon appears under each of the Skills that can be affected by a Resonance Mode Switch, to show the current Resonance Mode.

  • Click on the question mark beside the Resonance Mode Switch icon for more details.

Graphics & Performance

Extended the availability of the Frame Generation option on mobile

To enable the Frame Generation option, your device must meet one of the following requirements:

  1. Dimensity 9400 or 9400+ (not including tablet computers equipped with 9400e or 9400)

  2. Snapdragon 8 Gen 5

  • Access the Frame Generation option via Settings > Graphics > Frame Generation.

  • Enable this option to increase the frame rate while running the game.

For Dimensity 9400 or 9400+, you need to enable the Vulkan option first before enabling the Frame Generation option.

Please note that there is a frame rate limit on mobile devices produced by certain manufacturers.

Adjusted Graphics settings

  • Optimized the Special Effects Quality and Shadow Quality when the Graphics Quality is set to Ultra Performance, Performance, Balanced on some devices.

  • Widened the range of visuals affected by the LOD Bias option when enabled in the Graphics settings.

Due to variations in the in-game settings and system environment, enabling the option may occasionally cause performance issues, in-game glitches, and hardware-related issues. If you encounter issues, please try disabling the option. If the issue persists, please contact customer support for further assistance.

  • Added interactive effects to certain types of vegetation and sand in Lahai-Roi on PC.
