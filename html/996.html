New Area

Mt. Firmament

Beyond the southeastern sea of Jinzhou lies the secluded realm of Mt. Firmament. It is said that Sentinel Jué once graced this ancestral homeland of the Jinzhou people with its presence.

Now, however, this snowy peak is perpetually shrouded in mist, sealed away from commoners. Rumor has it that time flows eerily slowly there, almost as if it is stagnant...

Complete Main Quest Chapter 1 Interlude "A New Companion" to access the new area, Mt. Firmament.

An immature humanoid TD. Small amount of ice crystals is left on its body. It uses them to imitate the crisp sound of "clang bang" of ice colliding.
