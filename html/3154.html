During the event, 5-Star Resonator: Augusta, 4-Star Resonators: Chixia, Youhu, and Yuanwu receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

Version 2.6 update - 2025-09-17 09:59 (server time)

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the combat trial in ""First Resonance"" and activate the Convene function.

Convene Rules

  • [Eternal Radiance on the Crown] is a Featured Resonator Convene event banner. Use Radiant Tide to Convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or Weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The guarantee count is shared among all Featured Resonator Convene events and is carried over until a 5-Star Resonator is obtained.

  • Featured Resonator Augusta is exclusive to the [Eternal Radiance on the Crown] Featured Resonator Convene and will not be available by other means once the event ends.

  • Featured Resonator Augusta's Waveband will be simultaneously made available in the Afterglow Coral Store with a limit of 2 purchases, and it will be removed once the Convene event ends.

  • During the event, all the Resonators who receive boosted drop rates will become available in Ascendant Aces as Trial Resonators. Complete their Ascendant Aces challenges to obtain rewards.

Due to technical limitations, the rewards for completing 4-Star Featured Resonator Yuanwu's Ascendant Aces challenge in this version will be issued via in-game mail. Optimization will be made in this regard in a future version update. Eligibility: Create a character and unlock the Mail feature before 2025-08-28 04:00 (UTC+8); the rewards will remain claimable until 2025-09-17 09:59 (server time).

For more details, please refer to the in-game Convene page.
