Since the "Converging Paths" event's release, we have received your complaints about the sudden increase in resource requirements after unlocking Resonator Xiangli Yao and its added strain. To help ease your burden, we've decided to roll out a batch of special leveling events in Version 1.3 in addition to the content we originally planned:

Version 1.3 Special Leveling Events Explained

Alongside the events already announced, we'll add three leveling events—two brand-new and one rerun. Participate in these events to get a wealth of Shell Credit and various development materials. Don't miss out if you are at Union Level 14 or above!

NEW [Parade of Stars] Special Leveling Event

  • About: This event will feature 4 different types of tasks: Resonator Leveling, Weapon Leveling, Echo Leveling, and Skill Leveling. Complete these tasks to receive various rewards.

  • Duration: Version 1.3 update - 2024-11-13 15:59 (server time)

  • Rewards: Shell Credit x710,000, Premium Tuner x100, Premium Sealed Tube x50, Premium Resonance Potion x50, Premium Energy Core x34, Forgery Premium Supply x25, Premium Enclosure Tank x28, and other development materials.

"Forgery Premium Supply" allows you to select from: Heterized Metallic Drip, Flawless Phlogiston, Presto Helix, Waveworn Residue 239, and Cadence Blossom. "Premium Enclosure Tank" allows you to select from: Tailored Ring, FF Whisperin Core, and FF Howler Core.

NEW [Wuthering Exploration] Featured Exploration Event

  • About: Complete Survey Tasks daily to get Survey Points and claim rewards after meeting Survey Point requirements.

  • Duration: 2024-10-10 10:00 - 2024-10-24 03:59 (server time)

  • Rewards: Shell Credit x2,000,000, Tailored Ring x30, FF Whisperin Core x30, FF Howler Core x30.

RERUN [Wuthering Exploration] Featured Exploration Event: Mt. Firmament

  • About: Complete Survey Tasks daily to get Survey Points and claim rewards after meeting Survey Point requirements.

  • Duration: 2024-10-31 10:00 - 2024-11-13 03:59 (server time)

  • Rewards: Astrite x800, Malleable Elite Class Echo I x1, Malleable Elite Class Echo II x1, Premium Tuner x100, Premium Sealed Tube x30.

In the upcoming Version 1.3 [To the Shore's End], in addition to implementing the optimizations mentioned in the Version 1.3 Black Shores Broadcast, such as "Collection Spots Tracking" and "Batch Merge," we are also expanding the selection of development materials in the Store's Oscillated Coral section. Be sure to check them out after the update goes live!

We have also taken note of your other suggestions, such as "Echo info display upon absorption," "auto-select options for Echo upgrade," "portable synthesizer," and "Echo presets." These improvements are already in the works and will be rolled out in future updates. Please stay tuned!
