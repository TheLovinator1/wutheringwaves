Event Duration

2024/07/25 10:00 - 2024/08/08 03:59 (Server Time)

Rewards

Astrite* 400, Lustrous Tide*5, Premium Tuner, Crystal Solvent, Standard Weapon Mold, and other development materials.

Details

During the event, earn [Lollo Stamps] by completing daily event tasks.

Use one [Lollo Stamp] to receive a random "Lollo Helper" reward from the roulette.

The Special Delivery has three rounds. You'll unlock the next round after claiming all rewards from the current round.

Please note that roulette rewards cannot be claimed after the event ends, and the remaining [Lollo Stamps] will be cleared. Please check the end date and claim your rewards promptly.
