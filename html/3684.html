Maji, the idea king, is back with yet another new campaign. This time, he's riding the tech wave and trying to create his very own smart assistant, Lollo Genie.

Interact with adorable Echoes and submit their behavioural data to Maji to collect Lollo Stamps and participate in the thrilling lottery!

Duration

2025-12-08 04:00 - 2025-12-22 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x400, Lustrous Tide x5, Premium Tuner x100, Forgery Premium Supply x10, Premium Sealed Tube x15, Crystal Solvent x6, Advanced Enclosure Tank x12, Advanced Sealed Tube x24, Shell Credit x100000

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14 and complete Main Quest Chapter II Prologue ""Through the Sea Thou Break.""

Event Rules

  • During the event, you can complete Lollo Campaigns to obtain a set number of Lollo Stamps. Each Lollo Stamp lets you peel off one Lollo Campaign lottery ticket.

  • After the event ends, the unfinished Lollo Campaigns will not be available, and all the remaining Lollo Stamps will be recycled.
