During the event, 5-Star Weapon: Skull Thrasher, 4-Star Weapons: Relativistic Jet, Cadenza, and Fusion Accretion receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

Version 3.4 Update - 2026-07-09 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

  • Reach Union Level 5.

  • Or complete the combat trial in First Resonance.

  • Or access Main Quest Fast Track Event and proceed with the Main Quest until you reach a certain point.

Convene Rules

  • [Absolute Pulsation - Skull Thrasher] is a [Collab Weapon Convene] event of the [At Dream's Edge] collab campaign. Use [Shadowforge Tide] to Convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The Guarantee count is shared across all [Collab Weapon Convene] events under the same collab campaign. If a 5-Star Weapon is not obtained, the attempt is counted towards the Guarantee, which is carried over [Collab Weapon Convene] events under the same campaign and resets when a 5-Star Weapon is obtained.

  • In Collab Resonator Convene and Collab Weapon Convene of the same collab campaign, you receive [Illusive Coral] instead of [Afterglow Coral] upon obtaining 5-Star or 4-Star Resonators/Weapons. The Collab Convene rules differ from the Featured Convene rules to some degree; please refer to the in-game Convene page for details.

  • The 5-Star Weapon you Convene is guaranteed to be Skull Thrasher.

  • Featured Weapon Skull Thrasher is exclusive to the Collab Weapon Convene and will not be available by other means once the event ends.

  • During the event, your Collab Resonator Convene and Collab Weapon Convene attempts are tallied. When the tally reaches specific milestones, you can claim the corresponding rewards in Sweetdream Tuning. Please refer to the announcement regarding Sweetdream Tuning.

For more details, please refer to the in-game Convene page.
