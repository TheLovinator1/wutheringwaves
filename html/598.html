Qualification and Notification

  1. To participate in CBT2, please visit the CBT2 recruitment page and log in to your Kuro Account. Click the "Apply" button to proceed with the CBT2 Recruitment Questionnaire.

> > > Link: <https://wutheringwaves.kurogame.com/en/cbt>

  1. Please note that each Kuro Account can only submit the CBT2 Recruitment Questionnaire once. Once submitted, it cannot be edited.

  2. The CBT2 qualification is only valid for this test, and all game data will be cleared after the test ends.

  3. Once the recruitment is over, we will notify eligible participants who have qualified for CBT2 via email. You can also log in to the qualification inquiry page with your Kuro Account to check your qualification status. Please stay tuned for official updates regarding specific instructions for qualification distribution.

  4. Upon obtaining the CBT2 qualification, you will receive a download and installation guide for the preferred platform you have chosen in the Recruitment Questionnaire. If you selected multiple platforms in the Questionnaire, you will be assigned to one of the platforms. A confirmation of the designated platform will be provided in the email containing the download and installation instructions.
