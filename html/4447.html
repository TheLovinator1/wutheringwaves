[Adjustments and Optimizations]

Features

**

Expedition Motorbike**

Optimized Autopilot Mode

  • Adjusted the obstacle-avoidance logic in Autopilot Mode to reduce wrong turns while driving.

  • Removed Adaptive Hook prompt in Autopilot Mode.

**

Material Tracking**

Optimized Material Acquisition Process

  • Selecting the "Tacet Discord" acquisition path after clicking on the icon of a specific Material via Backpack or the Resonators screen now opens the map and tracks Tacet Discords that drop the material within a certain radius of your current location.

  • If there are no such Tacet Discords within the radius or all such Tacet Discords have been defeated, Tacet Discords outside the radius will be automatically tracked.

Leveling

**

Echoes:**

Optimized Echo Filtering

  • Refined the filtering logic and made the relevant interfaces more intuitive; the upgraded Echo filtering function applies to Echo-related interfaces, including Echo Backpack and Data Bank.

  • Added Batch Discard and Batch Lock, allowing you to Batch Discard or Batch Lock the filtered results.

Optimized Echo Backpack Interface and Functionality

  • Added a quick access button for Data Merge on the Echo Backpack interface.

  • Added Smart Discard, allowing you to discard owned Echoes based on Suggested Plan or Custom Management Plan.

  • More well-founded pop-up suggestions based on the number of Echoes you own and the number of Echoes you've discarded; you will be prompted to either Data Merge or Smart Discard Echoes when your Echo Backpack is full.

**

Echo Management:**

Optimized Echo Management

  • Adjusted the underlying logic of Echo management; plans can now be edited based on Sonata Effect, and when a a new Echo is obtained, it will automatically be locked or marked for discard according to the Mainstats of the COST4/COST3/COST1 Echoes under each Sonata Effect.

  • Added the Suggested Plans feature, allowing you to import recommended management plans with one click.

  • Added the Manage Owned Echoes feature, allowing you to batch discard or lock owned Echoes according to enabled current plans.

  • Added the Sharing & Importing feature, allowing you to share your Echo configurations by means of a Plan Code or import other Rovers' Echo configurations using their Plan Codes.

  • Added the Auto-Discard Non-5-Star Echoes feature, which, when enabled, will automatically mark for discard all non-5-Star Echoes you own or will obtain.

  • When resetting your plans, you can choose "Disable Only" or "Clear Plans and Disable" for all the Echo Management plans.

**

Enhanced Absorption**

Adjusted Enhanced Absorption Criteria

  • After defeating a Tacet Discord, the base Echo drop rate will be considered first, and if an Echo is dropped on account of the base drop rate, no Enhanced Absorption attempt will be deducted.

  • Guaranteed Echo drops during quests do not cost any Enhanced Absorption attempt.

Challenges, Puzzles, & Events

**

Ascendant Aces (Resonator Trial Event)**

Optimized Reward Claiming

  • You can now claim the rewards for Resonators whose trials you've completed in a previous version after entering the event, without having to repeat the challenges.

  • All the trials you've completed before the release of Version 3.2 are valid.

  • You can still try out a Resonator in Ascendant Aces even if you've completed their trial in a previous version.

Controls

  • Optimized the experience of reversing the expedition motorbike on mobile; when reversing is triggered, the joystick control area for reversing will be temporarily expanded.

  • Optimized the experience of using Expedition Motorbike-related buttons during certain quests/challenges; custom key bindings for the motorbike will no longer conflict with the buttons involved in these quests/challenges.

UI

**

Red Dot Notification**

Adjusted Red Dot Notification

  • Added a red dot to the access button for the Hazard Revisited event on the Events - Recurring Event page.

  • Added cross-device synchronization of red notification dots for certain screens. When you remove red dots from such screens as Featured Event, Resonators, Guidebook, and Expedition Motorbike from one device, the red dots will no longer appear on other devices.

  • Adjusted red dot notification on the Expedition Motorbike - Motorbike Appearance screen. You no longer need to click on icons to remove red dots from the Chassis Mod, Livery, and Motorbike Accessory pages; instead, when you obtain new Motorbike cosmetics, animated graphics will be displayed for the unlocked Motorbike cosmetics upon entering the Motorbike Appearance screen, and the red dots will be removed automatically.

More feedback is being collected on cross-device synchronization of red notification dots. This feature will apply to more screens in future versions.

  • Adjusted the display on Weekly Challenge-related screens; the number of remaining weekly attempts allowed for claiming rewards is now displayed instead.

  • Adjusted UI display during cutscenes. When no input is detected 3 seconds into a cutscene, story-related buttons, such as Skip and Story Review, will be hidden until input is detected.

  • Optimized the display for when certain location icons are selected on the Roya Frostlands map. After the Version 3.2 update, selecting certain location icons, such as Soliskin locations, on the Roya Frostlands map will allow you to switch between different layers of the current map.

Graphics & Performance

**

PC Graphics**

Added Render Distance on PC

  • You can adjust Render Distance via Settings > Graphics > Render Distance. Three Render Distance settings are currently available: Default, Far, Farthest.

  • The Far and Farthest settings may contribute significantly to the device load. Please choose a suitable setting based on the performance of your device.

  • Added interaction effects to some Celebration Balloons on PC.

**

Mobile Graphics**

Added High Quality on Mobile

  • Added High Quality as a graphics quality option on mobile above Quality.

  • Enabling this option will increase the clarity of in-game visuals on mobile.

  • To enable the High Quality option, your graphics card must meet one of the following requirements:

  1. Snapdragon 8 Elite or newer, or Snapdragon 8s Gen 4 or newer

  2. MediaTek Dimensity 9400 or newer

  3. XRing O1

After the Version 3.2 update, all mobile devices that support High Quality will have their default in-game resolution increased to some extent.

Due to variations in the in-game settings and system environment, enabling the option may occasionally cause performance issues, in-game glitches, and hardware-related issues. If you encounter any issue, please try switching to a lower setting. If the issue persists, please contact customer support for further assistance.

**

Graphics Settings**

Added Previews for Graphics Settings

  • A new button has been added to the right of certain graphics settings; click on it to view a text description of the effects that will be applied after enabling the setting.

  • A new button has been added to the right of certain graphics settings; click on it to bring up a visual preview of the effects that will be applied after enabling the setting.

Please note: The preview content is for demonstration purposes only; the actual in-game effects may vary to some extent from the previews shown.
