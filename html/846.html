During the event, boosted drop rates for 5-star Resonator [Yinlin], 4-star Resonators [Taoqi], [Aalto], and [Yuanwu]!

Event Period

2024/06/06 10:00 - 2024/06/26 11:59 (server time)

Participation Requirement

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the trial in "First Resonance".

Convene Rules

[When Thunder Pours] is a [Featured Resonator Convene] event type.

Use [Radiant Tide] to Convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-star or higher rarity Resonator or weapon every 10 attempts.

The Guarantee count is shared among all [Featured Resonator Convene] events. If a 5-star Resonator is not obtained, the Guarantee count is carried over any [Featured Resonator Convene] event and resets once a 5-star Resonator is obtained.

Featured Resonator [Yinlin] is exclusive to the [When Thunder Pours] Featured Resonator Convene and will not be available in other Convene types currently open.

During the event, [Yinlin's Waveband] will be available on [Store - Afterglow Coral] and will be removed from the Store after the event ends. You may purchase up to 2 limited-stock [Yinlin's Waveband].

For more details, please refer to the Convene screen in-game.
