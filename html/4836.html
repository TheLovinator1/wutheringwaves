During the event, 5-Star Resonator: Lucy, 4-Star Resonators: Lumi, Youhu, and Taoqi receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

Version 3.4 Update - 2026-07-09 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

  • Reach Union Level 5.

  • Or complete the combat trial in First Resonance.

  • Or access Main Quest Fast Track Event and proceed with the Main Quest until you reach a certain point.

Convene Rules

  • [Dreaming Upon the Moon] is a [Collab Resonator Convene] event of the [At Dream's Edge] collab campaign. Use [Dreamcatcher Tide] to Convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The Guarantee count is shared across [Collab Resonator Convene] events of the same collab campaign. If a 5-Star Resonator is not obtained, the attempt is counted towards the Guarantee, which is carried over across all [Collab Resonator Convene] events of the same collab campaign and resets once a 5-Star Resonator is obtained.

  • In Collab Resonator Convene and Collab Weapon Convene of the same collab campaign, you receive [Illusive Coral] instead of [Afterglow Coral] upon obtaining 5-Star or 4-Star Resonators/Weapons. The Collab Convene rules differ from the Featured Convene rules to some degree; please refer to the in-game Convene page for details.

  • Featured Resonator Lucy is exclusive to the [Dreaming Upon the Moon] Collab Resonator Convene and will not be available by other means once the event ends. Lucy's Waveband will not be made available in Item Exchange. You can obtain Lucy's Waveband by opening At Dream's Edge - Waveband.

  • During the event, Resonator Lucy will become available in the Resonator Trial event. Please refer to the [Night City Roaming] Featured Collab Event for details. Complete the challenge with a team that includes Lucy to claim rewards.

  • During the event, your Collab Resonator Convene and Collab Weapon Convene attempts are tallied. When the tally reaches specific milestones, you can claim the corresponding rewards in Sweetdream Tuning. Please refer to the announcement regarding Sweetdream Tuning.

For more details, please refer to the in-game Convene page.
