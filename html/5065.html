During the event, selectable 5-Star Resonators: Jiyan, Yinlin, Jinhsi, Changli, Zhezhi, and Xiangli Yao, and 4-Star Resonators: Buling, Sanhua, and Yangyang receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

Version 3.5 update - 2026-08-19 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

  • Reach Union Level 5.

  • Or complete the combat trial in First Resonance.

  • Or access Main Quest Fast Track Event and proceed with the Main Quest until you reach a certain point.

Convene Rules

  • [Starpath Reverbs] is a Reverb Resonator Convene event banner. Use Radiant Tide to convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or Weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The guarantee count is shared among all Reverb Resonator Convene events and is carried over until a 5-Star Resonator is obtained. Any guarantee count not resulting in a 5-Star Resonator in this Reverb Resonator Convene banner will carry over to the next Reverb Resonator Convene banner.

  • You can select one 5-Star Resonator within a designated range as the Featured Resonator of the event banner (you can re-select at any time). Every time a 5-Star Resonator is obtained, there is a 50% chance it will be the Featured 5-Star Resonator of this event banner, and a 50% chance it will be a random non-Featured 5-Star Resonator (not including the selectable Resonators for this event banner). All 5-Star non-Featured Resonators have equal drop rate. If the 5-Star Resonator obtained is not the Featured Resonator selected, the next 5-Star Resonator is guaranteed to be the Featured Resonator.

  • Selectable 5-Star Resonators: Jiyan, Yinlin, Jinhsi, Changli, Zhezhi, and Xiangli Yao.

  • The above selectable 5-Star Resonators' Wavebands will be made available in the Afterglow Coral Store with a limit of 2 purchases, and they will be removed once the Reverb Resonator Convene event ends.

Gifts of Starpath

  • During the event, Convene in the Reverb Resonator Convene to accumulate Convenes and unlock corresponding milestone rewards.

  • After the event ends, any unclaimed event rewards will be reissued via in-game mail if you log in within 30 days.

  • During the ""Blade of Past Resounds, Lingering Dream Hymns"" version period, a special event will be available: Reverb Resonator Convene's first 10x Convene is free. And the 10 free Convenes will also count toward the Gifts of Starpath's milestone count.

For more details, please refer to the in-game Convene page.
