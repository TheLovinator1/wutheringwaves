KU-Ryan, a Black Shores robot, is currently looking for help in gathering Surveillance Datasets on Waveworn Phenomena in Ragunna. The difficult landscape requires sharp reflexes and great grappling skills. If this sounds like your kind of mission, seek him out for more details.

Duration

2025-04-10 10:00 - 2025-04-24 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x400, Advanced Enclosure Tank x42, Forgery Advanced Supply x14, Advanced Sealed Tube x16, Advanced Resonance Potion x20, Shell Credit x420,000, and other Materials.

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14 and complete Chapter II Prologue ""Through the Sea Thou Break.""

Event Details

  • During the event, activate challenges across different regions.

  • After a challenge starts, Surveillance Datasets will appear. Try to collect as many Surveillance Datasets as possible within the time limit.

  • When all the datasets have been collected or when time runs out, you will receive rewards based on points obtained.

  • If there are any unclaimed rewards after the event ends, they will be automatically sent to your inbox when you log in again within 30 days.
