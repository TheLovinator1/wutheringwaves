You receive a new message from the Hunter's Den: Please examine the traces left by TDs and track them down.

Duration

2025-09-04 04:00 - 2025-09-22 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x400, Premium Resonance Potion x12, Premium Energy Core x8, Premium Sealed Tube x8, Forgery Advanced Supply x21, Advanced Enclosure Tank x42, Shell Credit x648888, and other Materials.

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14 and complete the first hunt in Main Quest Chapter II Act VIII ""By Sun's Burning Hand.""

Event Details

  • The event unlocks a new set of samples each day.

  • Each set contains samples that pose Easy, Normal, and Expert levels of challenge. Complete analyzing the corresponding samples to unlock the rewards.

  • To challenge the more difficult samples in the same set, you need to first complete the analysis of the previous sample.
