Version 2.0 Preview

New Region

Rinascita

Ragunna, in southern Rinascita, holds the closest bond with their Sentinel Imperator among the nation's many city-states. Most Ragunnesi worship the Sentinel, viewing Common Echoes as divine blessings. Ragunna is also home to the Order of the Deep, widely regarded as the Sentinel's earthly emissaries.

The Black Shores report that the Threnodian in Ragunna remains dormant. In recent years, however, even Sentinel Imperator has fallen silent, no longer speaking directly to the Ragunnesi...

When Version 2.0 goes live, Rovers who reach Union Level 14 and complete Main Quest Chapter I Act III "Ominous Star" can access Main Quest Fast Track "Next Stop: Rinascita" to unlock and participate in Main Quest Chapter II Prologue "Through the Sea Thou Break."

Adjustments and Optimizations

Resonators and Weapons

Outfits:

New Feature - Resonator Outfits

  • Give the current Resonator a new look by switching to a new Outfit.

  • Switching between Outfits does not change the Resonator's stats.

  • After switching to a new Outfit, you can equip the Resonator with the Weapon Projection associated with the Outfit.

[Injured] Visual Effects:

Added [Injured] Visual Effects

  • When Resonators have been in combat for over 20 seconds and have taken more than 7 hits, taking another hit triggers the [Injured] visual effects.

  • To remove the [Injured] visual effects, use Fast Travel or switch the current Resonator off the field and stay out of combat for 20 seconds.

Please note that in Version 2.0, the [Injured] visual effects are not available for Resonators who engage in combat wearing Outfits other than their original ones. We expect to include [Injured] visual effects for Resonator Outfits in Version 2.1.

Please note that the [Injured] visual effects is enabled by default in Version 2.0. We expect to add a toggle option for this feature in Version 2.1.

Features

Synthesis:

New Feature - Synthesis on the Go

  • Access Synthesis on your Terminal page.

  • Use Synthesis - Purification to quickly synthesize materials of different Rarities; you will have a clear view of the current quantities of the materials.

  • Use Synthesis - Conversion to convert two materials into one new material that is in the same category and of the same Rarity.

Change Appearance:

New Feature - Change Appearance

  • You can switch between the two Rovers via Settings > Other > Player Setting.

  • This feature has a 24-hour cooldown.

Immersion Mode:

New Feature - Immersion Mode

  • Enable Immersion Mode via Settings > Other > HUD Interface. Certain HUD information will be hidden.

  • Disable Immersion Mode with the keyboard shortcut or the icon in the top-left corner of the main screen.

Archive of Melodies:

New Feature - Archive of Melodies

  • Unlock in-game music tracks by delivering Melody Boxes to Aria Mummers.

  • Aria Mummers can play any track you select from your Archive of Melodies.

  • Set a selected track as the background music around Aria Mummer.

Exploration Progress:

Optimized Exploration Progress

  • Increase Exploration Progress in each region to unlock rewards.

  • Reach the required Exploration Progress percentage in a region to obtain an Exploration Report.

  • Track Quest, Challenge, or Item Collection progress for each region.
