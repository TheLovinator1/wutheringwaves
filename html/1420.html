During the event, 5-Star Resonator: Shorekeeper, 4-Star Resonators: Taoqi, Yangyang, and Chixia receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

Version 1.3 update - 2024-10-24 09:59 (server time)

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the combat trial in "First Resonance" and activate Convene function.

Convene Rules

  • [Till the Sea Turns Clear] is a Featured Resonator Convene event.

  • Use Radiant Tide to convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The guarantee count is shared among all Featured Resonator Convene events and is carried over until a 5-Star Resonator is obtained.

  • Featured Resonator Shorekeeper is exclusive to the [Till the Sea Turns Clear] Featured Resonator Convene and will not be available by other means once the event ends.

  • Featured Resonator Shorekeeper's Waveband will be simultaneously made available in the Afterglow Coral Store with a limit of 2 purchases, and it will be removed once the Convene event ends.

  • During the Convene, all Featured Resonators will be available in [Ascendant Aces] trial event. Complete the trial challenge with the Resonators to receive rewards.

For more details, please refer to the Convene screen in-game.
