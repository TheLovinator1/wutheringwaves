Yangyang: Xuanling

Born the second daughter of a renowned musical family in Mingting, she is gentle yet strong, pure of heart and romantic, a lady who draws her sword in the name of justice. As a "Votary of the Voice," she can commune with the Streams. Once an Outrider of the Midnight Rangers in Jinzhou, she now serves as an acting Xuan Watcher of Xuanfang Hold. Free as a breeze, bold in her chivalry, her life and her blade exist only to protect.
