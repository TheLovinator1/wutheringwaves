During the event, selectable 5-Star Weapons: Verdant Summit, Stringmaster, Ages of Harvest, Blazing Brilliance, Rime-Draped Sprouts, Verity's Handle, and 4-Star Weapons: Dauntless Evernight, Variation, and Endless Collapse receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

Version 3.5 update - 2026-08-19 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

  • Reach Union Level 5.

  • Or complete the combat trial in First Resonance.

  • Or access Main Quest Fast Track Event and proceed with the Main Quest until you reach a certain point.

Convene Rules

  • [Tideforge Reverbs] is a Reverb Weapon Convene event banner. Use Forging Tide to convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or Weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The guarantee count is shared among all Reverb Weapon Convene events and is carried over until a 5-Star Weapon is obtained. Any guarantee count not resulting in a 5-Star Weapon in this Reverb Weapon Convene banner will carry over to the next Reverb Weapon Convene banner.

  • You can select one 5-Star Weapon within a designated range as the Featured Weapon of the event banner (you can re-select at any time). Every time a 5-Star Weapon is obtained, it is guaranteed to be the Featured 5-Star Weapon selected.

  • Selectable 5-Star Weapons: Verdant Summit, Stringmaster, Ages of Harvest, Blazing Brilliance, Rime-Draped Sprouts, and Verity's Handle.

For more details, please refer to the in-game Convene page.
