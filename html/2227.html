During the event, 5-Star Weapon: Unflickering Valor, 4-Star Weapons: Waning Redshift, Comet Flare, and Marcato receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

2025-03-06 10:00 - 2025-03-26 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the combat trial in ""First Resonance"" and activate the Convene function.

Convene Rules

  • [Absolute Pulsation] is a Featured Weapon Convene event banner.

  • Use Forging Tide to convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or Weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The guarantee count is shared among all Featured Weapon Convene events and is carried over until a 5-Star Weapon is obtained.

  • The 5-Star Weapon you Convene is guaranteed to be Unflickering Valor.

  • Featured Weapon ""Unflickering Valor"" is exclusive to the Featured Weapon Convene and will not be available by other means once the event ends.

For more details, please visit the Convene page.
