During the event, 5-Star Resonator: Ciaccona, 4-Star Resonators: Aalto, Baizhi and Taoqi receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

2025-09-17 10:00 - 2025-10-08 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the combat trial in ""First Resonance"" and activate the Convene function.

Convene Rules

  • [Through Verses and Rhythms] is a Featured Resonator Convene event banner. Use Radiant Tide to Convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or Weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The guarantee count is shared among all Featured Resonator Convene events and is carried over until a 5-Star Resonator is obtained.

  • Featured Resonator Ciaccona is exclusive to the [Through Verses and Rhythms] Featured Resonator Convene and will not be available by other means once the event ends.

  • Featured Resonator Ciaccona's Waveband will be simultaneously made available in the Afterglow Coral Store with a limit of 2 purchases, and it will be removed once the Convene event ends.

  • During the event, all the Resonators who receive boosted drop rates will become available in Ascendant Aces as Trial Resonators. Complete their Ascendant Aces challenges to obtain rewards.

For more details, please refer to the in-game Convene page.
