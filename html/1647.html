[Adjustments and Optimizations]

Resonators & Weapons

  • Added an "Outline" option to the description pages of a Resonator's skills, allowing you to view a simplified version.

  • Optimized the description of Resonator Yinlin's skills. The damage type associated with Chameleon Cipher and Punishment Mark—both are features of Resonator Yinlin's Forte Circuit—is now specified. This adjustment does not affect the actual effects of her skills.

  • Optimized Resonator Yinlin's certain animations.

  • Optimized the text in the Information - Experience - Character Stories section for certain Resonators.

Features

Weapon:

(New) Weapon Projection

  • Change the appearance of a weapon to a selected Weapon Projection.

  • Equipping a weapon with a Weapon Projection does not change its stats.

  • Turn the equipped weapon completely or partially transparent using a Transparent Weapon Projection.

Participate in Somnium Labyrinth: Somnoire Adventure Event to obtain 4-Star Weapon Projection "Projection: Somnoire Anchor" for free. Participate in Version 1.4's Depths of Illusive Realm to obtain a complete set of Transparent Weapon Projections ("Projection: Formless" series) for free.

Map:

  • Added a feature that allows you to Fast Travel directly to the location of any activated Tacet Suppression or Boss Challenge via the Map.

  • Added a feature that allows you to track the Collection Spots for certain Ingredients and Herbs.

  • Added markers for the Hidden Entrances to certain underground locations in the Tiger's Maw - Mt. Firmament area.

  • Added the option that allows you to view the different Map tiers of certain underground locations in the Tiger's Maw - Mt. Firmament area.

  • Added on-screen icons that facilitate tracking. For example, when you select to track Shifang Pharmacy on the Map, a Shifang Pharmacy icon will appear on the screen, showing the general direction of the location and the distance.

Account:

Added the "Email Linking" option

  • Press the relevant icon on the main menu to visit the web page where you will be instructed to link your account to your Email.

  • After completing the process, return to the game to claim rewards (including Astrite).

Leveling

Echo Upgrade:

Step-by-Step Echo Upgrade

  • Conveniently upgrade an Echo to the exact levels that unlock new substats (5/10/15/20/25) through the Auto-Select Settings.

  • Select one of the two ways to add materials: "Only Echo EXP items" or "Echo EXP items and Echoes."

Challenge Rewards:

Added the "double rewards" option to Forgery, Simulation, or Tacet Suppression challenges.

  • You now have the option to spend double the Waveplates to earn double rewards upon completing a Forgery, Simulation, or Tacet Suppression challenge.

During the events Intensive Training, Bountiful Crescendo, or Chord Cleansing, you spend your Double Drop Attempts first to claim double rewards, and only after all the attempts have been spent do you start spending double the Waveplates to earn double rewards.

Challenges, Puzzles & Events

  • Added new Returnee Quest types to the event Reprise of Tides.

  • Adjusted the placement of enemies in Forgery Challenges and Simulation Challenges, making them stand closer to each other when they first appear.

  • Optimized the gameplay for Side Quest: Cerulean Echoes Adrift in the Winds.

  • Optimized the descriptions of certain Resonators' certain skills on the Resonator Upgrade page in Depths of Illusive Realm.

Controls

  • Added the "Preferences" option to the Settings - Controls page.

  • Adjusted the controller deadzone on iOS devices to make it consistent with those on PC and Android devices.

UI

  • Added a login pop-up displaying the numbers of Waveplates and Waveplate Crystals you own.

  • Added an Echo absorption pop-up. Upon absorption, the Echo's Sonata Effect category will be displayed along with its Mainstat and Substat.

  • Added text corresponding to the Outro Skill icon on the Resonators - Forte page.

  • Optimized the display of Results Screens that appear after challenges are completed, giving you a clearer view of the rewards you've earned.

  • Optimized the interface design regarding the Tower of Adversity, allowing you to view and adjust your team lineup without leaving the Tower.

  • Optimized the display of the RES stats of enemies in the Tower of Adversity, allowing you to view the multiple RES categories an enemy may possess.

  • Adjusted the rules under which red dots appear on the Resonators page.

  • Adjusted the order in which information is presented when the pull-down button for a Resonator's Skill is pressed on the Resonators - Forte page. Skill Detail differences between the current level and the next level now appear first.

  • Optimized the visuals of certain item icons.

  • Optimized the Chat button on Mobile, allowing customization via "Customize Keybinds."

Graphics & Performance

  • Added a feature that makes recommendations regarding Graphics Quality (accessed via Settings > Graphics > Graphics Quality). Recommendations are made based on the performance of your device and come in four options: Ultra Performance, Performance, Balanced, and Quality.

  • Added a process that instructs you to choose an image quality setting when you are creating a character for the first time. This setting can be adjusted afterward via Settings.

  • Added the "Auto FPS" option to the Graphics settings on Mobile.

With this option turned on, if your mobile device is overloaded and overheating, the maximum Frame Rate and maximum Graphics Quality will be lowered automatically to reduce the occurrence of lagging or other possible problems.

  • Optimized the game's performance on certain Android devices.

  • Adjusted the frame rate settings on Android devices. The "45 FPS" option has been changed to "40 FPS." Using the "40 FPS" option effectively reduces the setup load and minimizes the occurrence of overheating.

  • Optimized the in-game resolution settings on PC. Starting from Version 1.4, if an adjustment is made to the resolution settings via Settings > Graphics > Resolution, the latest adjustment will be applied when switching from Full Screen to Windowed Mode.

  • Optimized the shader compilation process on PC. This process no longer occurs immediately after the launching of the game. It now occurs on the title screen to reduce visual glitches, lagging, and other problems that could be caused by the compilation process.

Others

  • Adjusted the positions of certain NPC names on the screen to prevent them from overlapping with the NPCs.

  • Optimized the descriptions of certain in-game settings and functions.
