During the event, boosted drop rates for 5-Star Resonator: Xiangli Yao, 4-Star Resonators: Danjin, Aalto, and Yuanwu!

Duration

2024-09-07 10:00 - 2024-09-28 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the combat trial in First Resonance and activate Convene function.

Convene Rules

  • [Celestial Revelation] is a Featured Resonator Convene event.

  • Use Radiant Tide to convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The guarantee count is shared among all Featured Resonator Convene events and is carried over until a 5-Star Resonator is obtained.

  • Featured Resonator Xiangli Yao is exclusive to the [Celestial Revelation] Featured Resonator Convene and the [Converging Paths] event, and will not be available by other means once the events end.

  • Featured Resonator Xiangli Yao's Waveband will be simultaneously made available in the Afterglow Coral Store with a limit of 2 purchases, and it will be removed once the Convene event ends.

  • During the Convene, all Featured Resonators will be available in [Ascendant Aces] trial event. Complete the trial challenge with the Resonators to receive rewards.

For more details, please refer to the Convene screen in-game.
