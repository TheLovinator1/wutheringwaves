Dear Rovers,

Wuthering Waves will officially launch on Xbox on July 10, 2026 (UTC+8). To address common questions about linking your Microsoft account with your Kuro Games account and syncing your game progress, we've prepared the following FAQ. Please take a moment to review it to ensure a smooth start to your adventure in Solaris-3.

Xbox Game Pass Benefits

Q: After Wuthering Waves launches on Xbox, what are the requirements for claiming the Xbox Game Pass benefits pack?

A: Once Wuthering Waves is officially released, if the Microsoft account linked to your Kuro Games account has an active Xbox Game Pass subscription (including PC Game Pass), you can claim the benefits pack via "Terminal" > "Mail" in-game after linking your account and logging in. Please note that the portion of the benefits pack that refreshes with each version must be claimed by logging in with an account that has an active XGP subscription before the current version ends, or it will not be delivered.

Cross-Platform Progress Syncing

Q: Will my progress on different servers or platforms be shared under the same Kuro Games account after the Xbox launch?

A: Your progress will not be shared between different servers (e.g., Asia Server and Europe Server). However, within the same server, progress is synced across multiple platforms (PC, Android, iOS, Xbox, and PS5™).

If you start your journey on Xbox, you can later switch to PC or mobile platforms by logging in with the same Kuro Games account linked to your Microsoft account, carrying over your Xbox progress. Similarly, if you begin on PC or mobile, you can continue on Xbox using the same Kuro Games account to carry over your PC/mobile progress.

Q: How can I sync my game progress between Xbox and other platforms?

A: Please link your Microsoft account to the Kuro Games account you wish to use, and make sure you select the same server/region when logging in on other platforms, in order to enable cross-platform progress syncing.

Please note: If you're using a third-party login method such as Google or Facebook, you must first link a Kuro Games account email via "Settings" > "Account Settings" > "User Center" in-game before your progress can carry over properly. Simply verifying through the registration email of a third-party login method (e.g., Google or Facebook) will not complete progress synchronization — please keep this in mind.

Q: Which servers are available for the Xbox version of Wuthering Waves?

A: The Xbox version currently supports the Asia, Southeast Asia, Europe, America, and HMT (Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan) servers. The first time you log into the game on Xbox, you'll need to link your current Microsoft account to a server/region. Once linked, this cannot be changed again, and the link is only valid for the Xbox platform.

In-game Purchase

Q: Can I use items purchased on PC/Android/iOS platforms (Lunites, Lunite Subscription, etc.) on Xbox after linking my account?

A: Yes, once your accounts are linked, any items purchased on one platform will be displayed and accessible on all other platforms.

Q: Why hasn't my purchase from the Xbox Store/Web Store been delivered?

A: Items purchased through the Xbox Store or Web Store will be processed for delivery after you log into your game character. If you experience a delivery issue, please try the following methods in order:

Method 1: Restart the game and log back in to check for a delivery notification.

Method 2: Check "Terminal" > "Backpack" to see if the purchased items have arrived in your inventory.

Method 3: Verify that the Microsoft account you're currently logged into is the same one used to make the purchase.

If none of these methods resolve the issue, please contact customer support via "Terminal" > "Feedback" in-game.

Q: If I create a new game character on Xbox, can I make purchases for this character on PC/mobile?

A: Yes, you can make purchases for this character on PC, mobile, or through the official website.

Account Linking

Q: How do I link my Microsoft account to my Kuro Games account?

A: Depending on your situation, you can complete account linking using one of the following two methods.

Method 1: If you've already created a Kuro Games account, we recommend logging in with your existing account. The first time you enter Wuthering Waves on Xbox, select "Already have an account? Log in" and follow the prompts to link your Kuro Games account with your current Microsoft account.

Note: If you log in this way, we recommend setting a password for your Kuro Games account via "Settings" > "Account Settings" > "User Center" in-game to strengthen your account security.

Method 2: Create a new Kuro Games account using your current Microsoft account. The first time you enter Wuthering Waves on Xbox, select "Create a Kuro Games account" to log into the game.

Note: If you log in this way, we recommend linking an email and setting a password via "Settings" > "Account Settings" > "User Center" in-game, so you can access the same account on other platforms going forward.

Q: I've already played Wuthering Waves on PC/mobile and linked my Microsoft account to a Kuro Games account, but after entering the game I find my progress still isn't syncing?

A: The Kuro Games account linked to your Microsoft account isn't the same Kuro Games account you used on PC/mobile.

After logging in on Xbox, go to "Settings" > "Account Settings" > "User Center" in-game to check whether your currently linked account ID matches the one you use on PC/mobile.

If you need to log into Wuthering Waves with a different Kuro Games account, try the following methods:

Method 1: Create a new Microsoft account and re-enter the game to link it with the Kuro Games account used on other platforms.

Method 2: Request account unlinking support via "Terminal" > "Feedback" in-game.

Q: After playing on Xbox, how can I continue my Xbox progress on PC/mobile?

A: If you'd like to carry over your Xbox game progress to PC/mobile, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Make sure your Kuro Games account has a password set. You can set this via "Settings" > "Account Settings" > "User Center" in the Xbox version of the game.

  1. When you open the game on PC/mobile, log in using the email linked to your Microsoft account along with the password you set.

Q: How can I check which Kuro Games account is currently linked to my Microsoft account?

A: After logging into the game, you can check this via "Settings" > "Account Settings" > "User Center."

Q: After linking my Microsoft account to my Kuro Games account, can I unlink or change the linked account?

A: Yes. You can request to unlink your Microsoft account via "Terminal" > "Feedback" in-game. After unlinking, your progress under your Kuro Games account will not be lost, but it will no longer be shared with your Microsoft account. You will also no longer be able to access any game progress saved to that Kuro Games account via your Microsoft account. In addition, each Kuro Games account can only unlink a Microsoft account once, so please consider carefully before doing this.

Before switching linked accounts, we recommend linking an email or setting a password for your current Kuro Games account via "Settings" > "Account Settings" > "User Center," to avoid losing access to your account if you're unable to log in another way after unlinking your Microsoft account.

Q: Will I lose my existing game progress under my Kuro Games account after unlinking?

A: No. Unlinking your Microsoft account only removes the data sharing between your Microsoft account and your Kuro Games account. You can still use your Kuro Games account to log in on other platforms and access your existing game progress.

Q: If the game closes unexpectedly before the linking pop-up appears during my first login on Xbox, can I still link my accounts afterward?

A: Yes. If this happens, simply restart the game and follow the pop-up prompts to link your accounts.

Q: After linking my account, can I log in on other platforms (e.g., PC, Android, iOS) using my Microsoft account, or use a different login method on Xbox?

A: No. Currently, the Xbox version of Wuthering Waves only supports logging in with a Microsoft account linked to a Kuro Games account, and other platforms do not support logging in with a Microsoft account.

Q: Can my Microsoft account be linked to multiple Kuro Games accounts? Or can my Kuro Games account be linked to multiple Microsoft accounts?

A: No, each Microsoft account can only be linked to one Kuro Games account.
