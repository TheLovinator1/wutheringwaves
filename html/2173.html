The Water City has just unveiled a corner of its secrets. Now is the best time to set sail, ride the winds, and venture into the uncharted corners of this mysterious land.

With the world's curtain lifting, a new exploration calls! Dive into the adventure now!

Duration

2025-02-20 10:00 - 2025-03-10 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x400, Forgery Advanced Supply x12, Advanced Enclosure Tank x43, Advanced Sealed Tube x18, Advanced Resonance Potion x18, Shell Credit x570,000, and other materials.

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 22, complete ""Track down Gilberto"" in Main Quest ""Veils Off in Sun or Shadow,"" and unlock Utility: Flight.

Event Details

  • During the event, you can invite other players to join the Overdash Challenge in Co-op Mode. The first to finish is the winner.

  • Once the first player finishes, the challenge enters a 20-second final sprint phase. After the sprint phase ends, results will be finalized.

  • Combat is disabled during the challenge. Players must follow the markers to finish the challenge.

  • The final points consist of two parts: Course Completion Points and Ranking Points (Course Completion Points are earned by reaching designated checkpoints along the track, and Ranking Points are earned based on the order in which players finish.)

Reminder: Resonators of different heights run at different speeds.
