Details

  • [Fairy Tale's Finale] will gradually unlock 12 levels, consisting of 6 regular levels and 6 challenge levels.

  • In the team setup screen, you can choose ""Dorothy's Companions"" to assist you in battle as Support Echoes, significantly enhancing the combat abilities of Resonators. As the difficulty increases, you will face fiercer enemies and unlock more Support Echoes.

  • Downed Resonators will be automatically revived after a while.

  • You can earn Level Rewards by meeting the point requirement in regular levels. The total points is the sum of the highest points from all regular levels, and reaching the required total points will unlock additional Point Rewards. Clearing challenge levels will grant corresponding Level Rewards, but will not yield any points.

  • Co-op matching is available for all levels, allowing you to fight alongside other Rovers.
