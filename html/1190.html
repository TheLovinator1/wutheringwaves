Store Excess Waveplate:

New Feature to Store Excess Waveplate

  • Added the Waveplate Crystal count, and Waveplate Crystals can be exchanged for Waveplates of equivalent value.

  • The current counts of both Waveplates and Waveplate Crystals can be viewed on the Map screen.

  • Once you reach the Waveplate cap, you'll begin receiving Waveplate Crystals instead, which regenerate at a rate of 1 Crystal every 12 minutes.

New Resonator Showcase Feature

  • Tap the avatar on the Terminal screen to open the Resonator Showcase.

In shooting challenges or puzzles, you'll be able to use the new Hoverdroid: Shooter with any Resonator, instead of switching to a pistol-using Resonator.

Graphics & Performance

  • Optimized game performance for select mobile devices.

  • Added the Enemy On-Hit Material Effects options in Settings > Graphics.

  • The 120 FPS option will be available on select PC devices in Settings > Graphics.

To enable the 120 FPS option, both of the following system requirements must be fulfilled:

  1. Graphics Card: Supported NVIDIA RTX 30 series, RTX 40 series, or AMD RX 6000 series, RX 7000 series.

  2. CPU: Supported Intel or AMD model.

Depending on your system specifications and environment, enabling the 120 FPS option may cause performance issues or hardware errors. If you experience such problems, please disable the option and restart the game. If the issues persist, please send your feedback to Customer Service.

Meanwhile, we will continue to enhance game performance and address issues based on your feedback. We will expand support to more devices in future versions.
