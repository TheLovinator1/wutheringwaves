Participate to acquire 5-Star Resonator Xiangli Yao!

Duration

2024-09-04 10:00 - 2024-09-28 11:59 (server time)

Rewards

5-Star Resonator Xiangli Yao

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 17 and complete Main Quest Chapter I Act III Ominous Star.

Details

  • During Moon-Chasing Festival, participate in the [By Moon's Grace] event and prepare for the Moonlit Fair. You can boost the festival's Popularity by fulfilling Wishes and building Fair Stalls.

  • Once you reach the target Popularity, you can acquire 5-Star Resonator Xiangli Yao on the event page between 2024-09-07 10:00 and 2024-09-28 11:59 (server time).

  • If you have any unclaimed rewards when the event ends, you can log in within 30 days afterward to receive these rewards via mail.
