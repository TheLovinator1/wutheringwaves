Adjustments & Optimizations

Resonators & Weapons

Rover:

Optimized Rover's model appearance.

  • Optimized Rover's model materials and details.

  • Improved Rover's model appearance under various lighting conditions.

Tune Break:

Optimized the trigger mechanism of Tune Break.

  • Added a new way to trigger Tune Break: switching to another Resonator when Tune Break is available can still trigger the Tune Break interaction and refresh its duration.

  • Optimized the trigger method of Tune Break: when a target is in a state where Tune Break can be triggered, the Tune Break interaction can be triggered regardless of whether the target is locked-on. When an attack hits multiple enemies that are all in a state where Tune Break can be triggered, priority will be given to displaying the Tune Break interaction status of the locked-on enemy.

  • Optimized the interaction duration for triggering Tune Break.

  • Optimized the interaction range for triggering Tune Break, unifying the range for all Resonators.

  • Optimized the triggering of Tune Break in certain situations: Tune Break can now be triggered immediately after a Resonator casts their Resonance Liberation or Intro Skill.

  • Added an Accessory preview feature for Resonators. When selecting an accessory in the item details or the Backpack, you can click View to preview it.

  • Optimized the targeting mechanism of Basic Attacks automatically cast when switching Resonators.

System

Expedition Motorbike:

Added the feature to change vehicle models.

  • You can change vehicle models via the Expedition Motorbike - Motorbike Appearance interface.

  • The interface will display the description of the selected model and its supported Customizable Parts.

Added Boosted Drift Settings

  • You can enable this option via Settings - Controls - Vehicle settings.

  • Once enabled, you can drift with the Expedition Motorbike while in the Boost state, and the drift handling performance will be improved.

  • When riding the Expedition Motorbike in Mengzhou: Land of Xuanfang and encountering minor environmental obstructions, the motorbike will render partially transparent and continue driving normally through the obstruction.

We plan to improve the motorbike's behavior when passing through obstructions in a future version.

Synthesis:

Added the Bioprinting feature.

  • You can access the Bioprinting interface via Terminal - Synthesis - Bioprinting.

  • In this interface, you can perform Bioprinting by consuming the material Regenerative Pistils to produce specialty materials used for Resonator Ascension that are distributed across Huanglong: Jinzhou, the Black Shores, and Rinascita regions in previous versions.

  • The material Regenerative Pistil can be redeemed in the Oscillated Coral Store.

Echo & Enemy Gallery:

Added dynamic animations.

  • Echoes unlocked in the Data Bank - Echo Gallery interface will feature subtle dynamic animations.

  • Enemies unlocked in the Gallery - Enemies interface will feature subtle dynamic animations.

Auto-Play:

Optimized and adjusted the story Auto-Play feature.

  • During story segments, you can click the Auto-Play button in the top-right corner to select Semi-Auto mode or Full-Auto mode.

  • In Semi-Auto mode, the story will play automatically, but you must manually select dialogue options.

  • In Full-Auto mode, the story will play automatically. When a dialogue option appears, a countdown will be shown, during which you can still make a manual choice. If no choice is made or the mode is not switched before the countdown ends, the system will automatically select an option to progress the story.

WavesLine:

Optimized and adjusted the WavesLine system.

  • Added a click-to-speed-up feature. When chatting via Messages, you can click to speed up the chat bubble animations.

  • Added a Filter feature to WavesLine, allowing you to quickly filter contacts based on certain criteria.

  • Optimized the red dot notification for the WavesLine entry on the Terminal screen. The red dot at the entry will now be automatically cleared once you enter the WavesLine screen. (※If there are still unclaimed rewards in WavesLine, the red dot at the entry will remain.)

  • Optimized the sorting of messages. Messages with unclaimed rewards and unread messages will now be prioritized and displayed at the top.

Map

Optimized and adjusted certain map features and icon displays.

  • Added reward previews for Regional Gameplay of each area in Region Guide.

  • Optimized certain pathfinding logic. When clicking Go for a target located on a multi-layered map, the system will prioritize selecting the closest Resonance Beacon on the same layer. If there is no valid Resonance Beacon within range, the closest Resonance Beacon in straight-line distance will be selected.

  • Optimized certain icon display logic. Currently tracked icons will no longer be hidden when switching between other multi-layered maps.

  • Optimized the sorting of certain content on the Exploration Progress overview screen.

Progression

New Voyage Events:

Optimized and updated the novice events into New Voyage Events.

  • After the Version 3.5 update, Rovers who create a character and unlock the Event system will trigger a series of New Voyage Events.

  • New Voyage Events include New Voyage Login, A New Voyage, New Voyage Goals, Gifts of New Adventure, and New Voyage Aid. Among these, New Voyage Aid is a time-limited event, while the others are permanent events.

For more details and rewards, please refer to the in-game New Voyage Events page.

Graphics & Performance

Graphics Settings

Optimized and upgraded Intel GPU frame generation support to XeSS3

  • The following Intel GPUs support XeSS3:

Integrated Graphics: Intel processor integrated graphics with Iris Xe or Arc Graphics in their names.

Dedicated Graphics: All Intel Arc GPU series (A-series, B-series) and DG1 GPUs.

The game must be run in DX12 mode.

  • If the requirements are met, you can enable this feature under Settings - Graphics - Intel XeSS3. Once Intel XeSS3 is enabled, you can also select a frame generation multiplier from 2x, 3x, or 4x under the Frame Generation sub-option based on your hardware configuration and actual needs.

If the native frame rate is severely insufficient when XeSS3 frame generation is disabled, enabling the Frame Generation option can improve frame rates, but it may also introduce input latency and visual anomalies. Please use with caution.

  • After the Version 3.5 update, the Graphics Quality preset option in Graphics settings will affect the number of scene models in Mengzhou: Land of Xuanfang, and subsequent regions. If you experience stuttering or frame drops, please try lowering the Graphics Quality preset.

  • Added seasonal changes to certain scenes in Mengzhou: Land of Xuanfang.

  • Added visual feedback of raindrops interacting with Resonator models in rainy environments on PC.

  • Added floating fallen leaves on water surfaces in certain scenes of Mengzhou: Land of Xuanfang on PC.

  • Added interactive feedback when attacking specialty plants in certain scenes of Mengzhou: Land of Xuanfang on PC.

Audio

Resonator Voice Customization:

Added Resonator Voice Customization feature

  • You can download supported Voicelines and quickly switch between them via the voice selection pop-up under Resonator profile screen.

  • Click the settings button next to the voice options or go to Settings - Language to enter the Resonator Voice Customization editing page, where you can filter and centrally manage your voice settings.

  • Resonator Voice Customization settings will be automatically saved to the server and support multi-device synchronization.

  • You can use the Manage Voice Packs function on the editing page to download or clear voice resources. If the voice resource being cleared is in use by a character, that character will automatically fall back to the default voice.

To ensure a consistent story experience, Resonator Voice Customization settings only apply to non-story voicelines. Story voicelines will still match your selected global language.

Dodge Sound Effects:

Optimized and adjusted the trigger mechanism for consecutive dodge sound effects

  • The trigger interval for consecutive dodge sound effects has been extended to 20 seconds. If no dodge is performed within 20 seconds after a dodge, the consecutive dodge count will reset.

  • Being hit by an enemy will reset the consecutive dodge count.

  • Optimized and adjusted certain sound effects and stereo performance.
