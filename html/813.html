Event Duration

2024/05/30 04:00 - 2024/06/17 03:59 (Server Time)

Rewards

You can win up to Astrite* 360, Advanced Resonance Potion*9, Advanced Energy Core*9, Advanced Sealed Tube*18, and other development materials in this event.

Details

During this event, 6 tracks will be open for Tactical Hologram: Overdash. You can select any of the available tracks to challenge.

During the challenge, avoid traps within a limited time and head to the finish line as you collect Hologram Tokens along the track.

Once you reach the finish line, the challenge score will be calculated based on the number of Hologram Tokens obtained. Higher scores grant better rewards!
