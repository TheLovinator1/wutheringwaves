During the event, the qualified Rovers can claim rewards through login or quests.

Duration

After the 1.2 update

Eligibility

  • Reach Union Level 8.

  • Haven't logged in for at least 30 consecutive days.

  • At least 30 days have passed since the last [Reprise of Tides] ends.

[Reprise of Tides] event will activate when all the above conditions are met and will last for 14 days.
