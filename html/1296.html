The Pioneer Association has decided to focus on the Central Plains in the next issue of Wutherium Geography. To this end, it has organized a special exploration in the Central Plains.

Duration

2024-09-12 10:00 - 2024-09-26 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x800, Malleable Elite Class Echo I x1, Malleable Elite Class Echo II x1, Premium Tuner x100, Premium Sealed Tube x30.

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14.

Details

  • Complete Exploration Quests daily to get Exploration Points and claim rewards after achieving Exploration Point milestones.

  • Exploration Quests refresh every day at 4:00 (server time). Unclaimed Exploration Points will be automatically claimed at this time.

  • If you have any unclaimed rewards when the event ends, you can log in within 30 days afterward to receive these rewards via mail.
