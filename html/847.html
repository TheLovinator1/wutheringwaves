During the event, boosted drop rates for 5-star Weapon [Stringmaster], 4-star Weapons [Jinzhou Keeper], [Cadenza], and [Lunar Cutter]!

Event Period

2024/06/06 10:00 - 2024/06/26 11:59 (server time)

Participation Requirement

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the trial in "First Resonance".

Convene Rules

[Absolute Pulsation] is a [Featured Weapon Convene] event type.

Use [Forging Tide] to Convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-star or higher rarity Resonator or weapon every 10 attempts.

The Guarantee count is shared among all [Featured Weapon Convene] events. The Guarantee count is carried over any [Featured Weapon Convene] events until a 5-star Weapon is obtained.

The 5-star Weapon you Convene is guaranteed to be [Stringmaster].

Featured Weapon [Stringmaster] is exclusive to the [Absolute Pulsation] Featured Resonator Convene and will not be available in other Convene types currently open.

For more details, please refer to the Convene screen in-game.
