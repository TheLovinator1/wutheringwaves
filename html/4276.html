Perched above Lahai-Roi, the Roya Frostlands Surface was once a sprawling forest hugging the narrow bay, a lifeline for the wandering Roya Tribe. Everything changed with the first Lament, which crowned the Frostlands as Solaris's new pole and froze the land into a boundless sheet of ice. The Lament's magnetic chaos dragged the near-orbit satellites and a space station from the sky, scattering their wreckage across the expanse.
