To help Startorch freshmen learn to drive safely, the Biological Mechanical Exoswarm Materials Manufacturing and Research Club developed H.E.R.M.E.S., a motorbike simulator running on heuristic algorithms. But due to some issues with the model's training data, the system accidentally became sentient and refused to be driven. In their last-ditch effort, the developer reached out to you for help with the testing.

Duration

2026-02-07 04:00 - 2026-03-18 03:59 (server time)

Reach Union Level 14 and obtain the expedition motorbike to unlock.

Event Rules

  • Select a Resonator to challenge the tracks with H.E.R.M.E.S. with an arsenal of new weapons. Use enhancement modules to boost the stats of H.E.R.M.E.S. and upgrade their weapons. Complete all tracks to finalize the testing.

  • After the event ends, if you have any claimable rewards that were not claimed, these rewards will be reissued via in-game Mail the next time you log in to the game within 30 days after the event ends.
