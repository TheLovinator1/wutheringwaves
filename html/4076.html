Starward Riseway

A colossal lift linking Lahai-Roi to the Roya Frostlands, its structure resembling a spine piercing both heaven and earth. Powered by orbital-acceleration technology, it plummets and ascends through the deep cavern, serving as one of Lahai-Roi's most important transportation hubs.

Lahai-Roi

Hidden beneath the frozen crust of Roya Frostlands, Lahai-Roi rose as Solaris' new pole after the devastating strike of Lament.

The subterranean world blossomed with strange landscapes, fostering life and civilization, all aglow in the light of the Exostrider's Drive.

Bjartr Woods

A sacred homeland of the Roya and the habitat of the Soliskin. Bathed in the radiance of the Reactor Drive, its towering trees reach hungrily toward the light, their deep, interwoven roots returning that energy to the earth.

Stagnant Run

The river valley was once alive with thousands of circling fish and the rolling boil of rays in flight. Now, its waters have grown stagnant, reduced to a swamp-like pool where even giants can no longer tread.
