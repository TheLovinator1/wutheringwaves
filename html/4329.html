During the event, 5-Star Weapon: Daybreaker's Spine, 4-Star Weapons: Hollow Mirage, Jinzhou Keeper, and Overture receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

2026-02-26 10:00 - 2026-03-18 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

  • Reach Union Level 5.

  • Or complete the combat trial in First Resonance.

  • Or access Main Quest Fast Track Event and proceed with the Main Quest until you reach a certain point.

Convene Rules

  • [Absolute Pulsation] is a Featured Weapon Convene event banner.

  • Use Forging Tide to convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or Weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The guarantee count is shared among all Featured Weapon Convene events and is carried over until a 5-Star Weapon is obtained.

  • The 5-Star Weapon you Convene is guaranteed to be Daybreaker's Spine.

  • Featured Weapon Daybreaker's Spine is exclusive to the Featured Weapon Convene and will not be available by other means once the event ends.

For more details, please refer to the in-game Convene page.
