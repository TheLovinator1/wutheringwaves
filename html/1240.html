Dear Rovers,

First, we want to express our heartfelt thanks for all your support and love for Wuthering Waves. It's crazy how time flies—we are already halfway through Version 1.2 "In the Turquoise Moonglow". We hope the Moonlit Fair has been a blast for you so far.

While you are enjoying all the festivities, new stories are unfolding beyond the borders of Jinzhou. We have some awesome updates in the pipeline and we are excited to give you a sneak peek through this preview.

In this preview, you can have a glimpse into the new area, gameplay features, and some storylines currently in development.

New Area

New Area: The Black Shores

The Black Shores, an archipelago enshrouded in mysteries and turmoil, remains sealed away from the world in thick digital fog. It is still uncertain when or who will lift its veil, allowing it to once more shoulder the task of connecting the world.

New Character

New Resonator: Youhu

We've cooked up something special with our upcoming Resonator Youhu. Her Resonance Skill and Resonance Liberation come with new QTE mechanics that bring a twist to combat. It's fun, it's unique, and we think you're going to love it.

New Gameplay Content

New "Portal" Puzzles

We are adding "Portals" to some puzzle-solving scenarios so you can hop between different areas, or even... No more spoilers, we can't wait to see how you use them!

New Special Enemy: Hedgepuff

Complete the new collection quests in the Black Shores to unlock this little guy and other special enemies. Keep an eye out for them—they are scattered across the shores, waiting for Rovers brave enough to test their skills.

New Boss: Fallacy of No Return

A ██████ created by the Tethys System, patrolling the borders between reality and digital world.

New Tactical Hologram: Inferno Rider

We are also expanding the Tactical Hologram with some exciting new challenges. Even more exciting and challenging battle content will be coming your way in future updates. Be ready!

New Stories

New Companion Story: Small Wooly, Big Adventures

Every story is an extension of life, and adventure is the beginning of a story. Encore extends a special invitation to you—the adventure is about to begin! As her closest friend, will you join the Wooly Warrior on this exciting journey?

Note: All content shared in the preview is still in development and may not represent the final quality. For accurate details, please refer to the official release notice when the content goes live.

There is still so much more on the horizon, and we will continue to share new content and optimization details with you, so stay tuned!
