Dear Rovers,

Starting with Wuthering Waves Version 3.5, XBOX Game Pass members can claim exclusive in-game rewards!

  • Version 3.5 will be released on July 10th, 2026 (UTC+8).

Rewards

XBOX Game Pass Gift Pack (Once per Version):

Morphable Echo x2, Premium Tuner x10, Shell Credit x30,000

XBOX Exclusive Sigil: "Paths and Possibilities" (One-Time Reward):

An exclusive Sigil available only to XBOX Game Pass members. Once claimed, it will be permanently added to your account.

How to Claim

XBOX Game Pass Gift Pack:

After each version update, XBOX Game Pass members can log in to the XBOX Series X|S or XBOX PC version of Wuthering Waves and claim the Gift Pack via in-game mail.

XBOX Exclusive Sigil:

After updating to Version 3.5, XBOX Game Pass members can log in to the XBOX Series X|S or XBOX PC version of Wuthering Waves and claim the exclusive Sigil via in-game mail. This is a one-time reward.

Notes

You must reach Union Level 2 and unlock the Mail feature to claim these rewards.

Each UID can claim one XBOX Game Pass Gift Pack per version.

Each UID can obtain the XBOX Exclusive Sigil only once. It cannot be claimed again.

Reward delivery may be delayed in some cases. If you do not receive the rewards, please try logging in again later.

If you have any questions, please contact our official customer support.
