Dear Rover,

To enhance your gameplay experience, we will be upgrading the Reprise of Tides Returnee Event in Version 2.3. As part of this update, the event will be temporarily unavailable for maintenance. Please take note of the scheduled maintenance time, and we appreciate your understanding for any inconvenience caused.

For details about the improved Reprise of Tides Returnee Event, please refer to the upcoming official announcements.

Maintenance Schedule

2025-04-14 04:00 (server time) - Version 2.3 update

Requirements for Participation in Reprise of Tides

  • You have reached Union Level 8.

  • You have not logged in for at least 30 consecutive days.

  • At least 30 days have passed since the end date of the last Reprise of Tides event that you participated in.

Rovers who meet the above requirements can activate Reprise of Tides, which will last for 14 days upon activation.

Regarding the Accessibility of Reprise of Tides

If you activate Reprise of Tides before maintenance starts, your access will remain unaffected.

If you meet the returnee requirements during the maintenance period, you will temporarily be unable to assess the event until maintenance is over. Access will be granted to the updated Reprise of Tides Returnee Event once Version 2.3 goes live.
