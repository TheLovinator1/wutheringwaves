Maji, your entrepreneurial friend, has made his way across the sea to Ragunna. It seems he's stumbled upon a golden opportunity, and he's eager to get you on board.

Lollo Stamps can be exchanged for Lollo Helper special deliveries.

Duration

2025-04-10 10:00 - 2025-04-24 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x400, Lustrous Tide x5, Premium Enclosure Tank x5, Forgery Premium Supply x10, Crystal Solvent x5, Shell Credit x340,000, and other Materials.

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14.

Event Details

  • During the event, complete Lollo Campaigns to obtain Lollo Stamps.

  • Each Lollo Stamp can be exchanged for a Lollo Helper special delivery.

  • There are 3 Inventories for the Lollo Helper special deliveries. Once you collect all deliveries in an Inventory, the next Inventory will be unlocked.

  • If you win the Lollo Express special delivery service, you can claim all deliveries in the current Inventory.

After the event ends, the unfinished Lollo Campaigns will not be available, and all your remaining Lollo Stamps will be recycled. Please make sure to use your Lollo Stamps and claim your rewards before the event ends.
