The Academy is experimenting with a new type of technology rumored to be capable of simulating powerful enemies to help with the troops' combat training...

Recently, the project was able to incorporate fresh data samples collected from Rinascita. A new round of testing is in the works...

Duration

2025-02-27 10:00 - 2025-03-13 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x600, Malleable Elite Class Echo III x1, Malleable Elite Class Echo IV x1, Premium Tuner x100, Premium Sealed Tube x21, Shell Credit x660,000, and other materials.

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14.

Details

  • In Tactical Simulacra Ⅲ, you will challenge waves of powerful enemies by using special buffs across different simulation domains.

  • A total of six stages will be available in Tactical Simulacra Ⅲ. Each stage will unlock once you reach 1,000 points in the previous stage.

  • As the difficulty rises, you will face consecutive battles with more enemy bosses, but will also be granted more buffs.

  • The final score of each challenge is calculated based on Monster Points, Time Points, and Technical Points. Reach the required score in each stage to earn corresponding rewards.

About Combat Rank: Hitting the enemy steadily improves your Combat Rank, while successful Dodges, Dodge Counters, and Counterattacks provide a greater boost. However, getting hit significantly lowers your Combat Rank.
