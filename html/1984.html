Pioneer Association picked the Nimbus Sanctum as the new theme for Wutherium Geographic magazine. The Association has set up a special survey for the Nimbus Sanctum.

Duration

2025-01-16 10:00 - 2025-01-30 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Premium Tuner x300, Premium Sealed Tube x60, Shell Credit x500,000.

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14.

Details

  • Complete Survey Tasks daily to get Survey Points and claim rewards after meeting Survey Point requirements.

  • Survey Tasks refresh every day at 4:00 (server time). Unclaimed Survey Points will be automatically claimed at this time.

  • If you have any unclaimed rewards when the event ends, you can log in within 30 days afterward to receive these rewards via mail.
