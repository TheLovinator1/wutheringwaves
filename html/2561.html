Echo Aspect

New Feature: Echo Aspect

  • This feature allows you to apply Glide or Flight Aspects to your Resonators.

  • You can apply an Echo Aspect to the current Resonator or all the Resonators.

  • Glide or Flight Aspects do not affect Resonators' performance.

Features

Profile

New Feature: Birthday Blessing

  • Set your date of birth via Terminal > Profile > Set Birthday.

  • Once you've set your date of birth and reached Union Level 2, the Birthday Blessing feature will be unlocked.

  • Log in on the day of your birthday or within 7 days after to receive a unique birthday surprise.

  • If you log in after the 7 days have passed but still within 30 days after your birthday, the gift will still be sent to you via Mail.

  • If your birthday has already passed when you finish the initial birthdate setting, you can still receive the birthday blessings and gift for the current year via in-game mail.

  • You can return to the birthday space anytime by using the Supermassive Birthday Cake in your Backpack.

  • You can go to your Backpack to put on the special birthday gift "The Stars that Shine For You."

Please note that your date of birth cannot be changed once set. Be sure to enter the correct date.

Graphics & Performance

Graphics Settings

Broader Ray Tracing Support

  • In addition to the RTX 40 series and RTX 50 series, Ray Tracing will become available for the following NVIDIA graphics cards:

  1. RTX 2070 and newer from the RTX 20 series.

  2. RTX 3060 and newer from the RTX 30 series.

  • Ray Tracing will also be available for AMD graphics cards that meet the following requirements:

  1. Minimum 10 GB VRAM

  2. Hardware support for Ray Tracing

Excluding 6700, 6700M, 6700 XT, 6700S, 6750 XT, 6750 GRE, RX 7600, 7600M, 7600 XT, 7600M XT, 7600S, and 7650 GRE.

The Dev Team is currently working to expand Ray Tracing support to the RX 9000 series. Stay tuned to official announcements for updates on the availability.

  • Ray Tracing will also be available for Intel graphics cards that meet the following requirements:

  1. With at least 10 GB of VRAM

  2. With hardware that supports Ray Tracing

  • Added system checks and prompts regarding your device's ability to support Ray Tracing.

  • Added an On/Off option for Ray-Traced Shadows.

  • Optimized the performance of the game with Ray Tracing enabled.

  • Optimized the effects of Ray-Traced Reflections when Ray Tracing is enabled.

Wider 120 FPS Support

  • In addition to the RTX 40 series and RTX 50 series, the 120 FPS option will become available for the following NVIDIA graphics cards:

  1. RTX 2070s and newer from the RTX 20 series.

  2. RTX 3060 and newer from the RTX 30 series.

  • Made the 120 FPS option available for AMD RX 6600 XT or newer.

Please note that certain CPU requirements, in addition to the above GPU requirements, need to be met for the 120 FPS option to work.

Due to variations in the in-game settings and system environment, enabling the 120 FPS option may occasionally cause performance issues, in-game glitches, and hardware-related issues. If you encounter any issue, please try disabling the 120 FPS option. If the issue persists, please contact customer support for further assistance.

  • Added the Frame Generation option for mobile devices equipped with Snapdragon 8 Elite.

  • Improved game resolution and visual performance for iPads equipped with M1 or newer processors.

Pre-download

Added Mobile Pre-download

When pre-downloading for the latest version becomes available, press the ""Pre-download"" button, either on the title screen after a successful login or on the in-game Terminal page to pre-download update resources.

Selective Download

Added Mobile Selective Download

  • This feature is at the testing stage and will be available only on some Android and iOS devices.

  • Select between Full Resources and Basic Resources after installing or reinstalling the game.

  1. Full Resources: Download all the available resources in one go.

  2. Basic Resources: Basic Resources include only story resources for the early game. The remaining resources may be downloaded in the background as you play in the game.

Whether or not a selective download panel appears, the game will function normally once the required resources have been downloaded.

The options Full Resources and Basic Resources differ only in whether the cutscenes (MP4) are downloaded. They have no effect on your experience of the base game.

  • Added a panel to the title screen that appears when you download resources for the first time or when the size of the current download is ≥500MB. The panel shows the details of the content that will be downloaded.
