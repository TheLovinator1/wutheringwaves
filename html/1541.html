During the event, 5-Star Resonator: Jiyan, 4-Star Resonators: Youhu, Sanhua, and Mortefi receive boosted drop rates!

Duration

2024-10-24 10:00 - 2024-11-13 11:59 (server time)

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 8 or complete the combat trial in ""First Resonance"" and activate Convene function.

Convene Rules

  • [Prevail the Lasting Night] is a Featured Resonator Convene event.

  • Use Radiant Tide to convene. You are guaranteed at least one 4-Star or higher rarity Resonator or weapon every 10 attempts.

  • The guarantee count is shared among all Featured Resonator Convene events and is carried over until a 5-Star Resonator is obtained.

  • Featured Resonator Jiyan is exclusive to the [Prevail the Lasting Night] Featured Resonator Convene and will not be available by other means once the event ends.

  • Featured Resonator Jiyan's Waveband will be simultaneously made available in the Afterglow Coral Store with a limit of 2 purchases, and it will be removed once the Convene event ends.

  • During the Convene, all Featured Resonators will be available in [Ascendant Aces] trial event. Complete the trial challenge with the Resonators to receive rewards.

  • Starting from Version 1.4, 4-Star Resonator Youhu will be permanently available in Standard Weapon & Character Convene.

  • 4-Star Resonator Youhu will be simultaneously made available in the Afterglow Coral Store and will be removed at the end of Version 1.4.

4-Star Resonator Youhu costs 80 Afterglow Corals and can only be purchased once from the Afterglow Coral Store. If you already own the Resonator, you will get Youhu's Waveband instead.

For more details, please refer to the Convene screen in-game.
