At this year's Club Show & Tell, Van Tassie of the Psychological Threshold Alternative Therapy Society proudly unveils his latest project: the ""F.U.E.L. Optimization Simulator."" He claims this game will revolutionize the Academy's culinary experience by gathering the essential data. As is par for the course, your name is on the playtest roster. Yet, something about the project's core design seems... slightly off from the outset.

Duration

2026-04-02 04:00 - 2026-04-20 03:59 (server time)

Rewards

Astrite x400, Forgery Advanced Supply - Lahai-Roi x14, Advanced Enclosure Tank - Lahai-Roi x28, Advanced Resonance Potion x20, Advanced Energy Core x20, Advanced Sealed Tube x16, Shell Credit x280000

Eligibility

Reach Union Level 14 and activate the Resonance Nexus in Startorch Academy during Main Quest Chapter III Act I ""What Burns Beneath Frostlands"" to unlock.

(For the best experience, please complete this event's preceding quest ""Gold Suspended in Shadows."")

Event Rules

  • Access the event from the Mind Dive Arcade machine or the Event Page. Control your Resonator to move left and right to catch falling food and earn Gourmet Points.

  • Catch food to build Energy. Once your Energy bar is full, you can activate a powerful Resonator Skill. Be careful—catching certain negative foods will drain your Energy and slow you down.

  • Reach the target Gourmet Points to earn 1 Star to clear the stage. Quitting the game midway will finalize this stage based on the Gourmet Points earned.

  • Stage 1 is available when the event begins. A new stage unlocks each following day, each featuring a new Resonator with unique skills.

  • Any unclaimed rewards will be sent to your in-game mailbox automatically if you log in within 30 days after the event ends.
